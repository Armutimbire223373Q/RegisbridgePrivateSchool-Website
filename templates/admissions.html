Application Process

1

Submit Application

Complete and submit your online application form with all required documents.

2

Document Review

Our admissions team will review your application and supporting documents.

3

Interview

Schedule and attend an interview with our admissions committee.

4

Decision

Receive your admission decision within 2-3 weeks of your interview.

Admission Requirements

Primary School

  • Birth certificate
  • Immunization records
  • Previous school records (if applicable)
  • Parent/Guardian identification
  • Proof of residence
  • Application fee payment

Secondary School

  • Birth certificate
  • Primary school completion certificate
  • Academic transcripts
  • Character reference letter
  • Medical examination report
  • Application fee payment
